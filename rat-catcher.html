Signs of a Rodent Infestation

  • Droppings near food sources or along paths.
  • Damaged wires or furniture.
  • Nests constructed from shredded paper or fabric.
  • Strange sounds in walls or attics, especially at night.

If you detect any of these signs, it's vital to act promptly to prevent a worsening infestation.
